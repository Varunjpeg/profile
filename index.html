Who I Am

I'm Varun, a second-year B.Tech student at VIT Chennai pursuing Electronics Engineering with a specialization in VLSI Design and Technology. I'm passionate about hardware — from designing chips to building smart embedded systems.

My work spans FPGA-based digital design, embedded IoT, AI-integrated signal processing, and semiconductor device simulation. I thrive at the boundary between hardware and intelligence.

Outside the lab, I lead and collaborate across student communities — from IEEE EDS to The White Helmets — because I believe the best engineers are also great communicators and team-builders.
